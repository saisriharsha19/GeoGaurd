What is GeoGuard?

GeoGuard is an intelligent browser extension that protects your location privacy whenever websites request your geographical coordinates. Unlike simple location blockers, GeoGuard uses context-aware privacy mechanisms that adapt to your privacy needs while maintaining functionality on location-based services.

How It Works

GeoGuard uses machine learning to understand your location patterns and identify sensitive areas like your home or workplace. When a website requests your location, GeoGuard creates a privacy-preserving version of your coordinates that keeps you within the general area but protects your exact position.

1

Intercept Location Requests

GeoGuard safely intercepts location requests from websites before they get your real coordinates.

2

Apply Context-Aware Privacy

Our backend analyzes the location to determine if it's sensitive, then applies the appropriate level of protection.

3

Return Protected Coordinates

The website receives modified coordinates that protect your privacy while still enabling location-based features.

Getting Started

GeoGuard is ready to use with default settings, but you can customize it to fit your privacy needs. The extension is active by default with a medium privacy level (5) that protects all websites.

1

Check Your Backend Server

Make sure your Python backend server is running at http://localhost:5000 (or configure a different address in settings).

2

Adjust Privacy Level

Click the GeoGuard icon and use the slider to adjust your preferred privacy level from 1 (minimal) to 10 (maximum).

3

Configure Protected Websites

Choose to protect all websites or only specific domains in the extension settings.

Open GeoGuard Settings

GeoGuard is designed to enhance your online privacy. While it provides strong location privacy protection, no solution is completely perfect. For the highest level of privacy, consider disabling location services entirely when not needed.
